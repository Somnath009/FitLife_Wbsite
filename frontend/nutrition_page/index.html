Fuel Your Goals

Clean bulking, fat‑loss, or cutting—build plates with the right mix of protein, smart carbs, and healthy fats.

Weight Gain

Choose calorie‑dense whole foods to hit a small surplus and support lean mass. Think nuts, dairy, whole grains, avocado, and hearty proteins.

Avocado + Whole‑grain Toast

Energy‑dense monounsaturated fats with fiber‑rich carbs; easy add‑on for surplus calories.

Healthy fats

Nut Butters + Banana

Peanut/almond butter adds calories and protein; pair with fruit or shakes for clean bulk.

Calorie‑dense

Greek Yogurt Parfait

High‑protein dairy topped with granola and mixed nuts for extra calories and crunch.

Protein

Chicken + Rice Bowl

Lean protein with carb base; add olive oil or ghee for surplus and flavor.

Balanced

Examples align with clean bulking guidance favoring whole‑food calories like nuts, dairy, avocados, rice, and protein sources. [Refs]

  • High‑calorie whole‑food options for gaining. [3][18]
  • Snack add‑ons like nuts, yogurt, eggs, and shakes. [6]

Weight Loss

Prioritize high‑protein, high‑fiber meals to stay full in a small deficit—lean meats, fish, legumes, yogurt, veggies, and whole grains.

Grilled Salmon + Greens

Protein with omega‑3s supports satiety and cardiometabolic health in calorie control.

Protein • Omega‑3

Greek Yogurt + Berries

High‑protein dairy plus fiber and polyphenols for a filling snack or breakfast.

High‑protein

Bean + Quinoa Bowl

Plant protein and fiber‑rich grains help reduce hunger and support steady energy.

Protein • Fiber

Chicken Salad Wrap

Lean poultry with veggies; choose whole‑grain wrap and light dressing for volume eating.

Lean protein
  • High‑protein foods aiding weight loss: poultry, fish, yogurt, legumes, tofu. [7][16]
  • High‑fiber foods help fullness and appetite control. [19]

Cutting

Hold protein high to maintain muscle, use complex carbs for training, and include healthy fats—simple, consistent meals work best.

Egg Scramble + Veg

Complete protein and micronutrients; low‑calorie volume with spinach, peppers, mushrooms.

Protein

Turkey + Quinoa + Broccoli

Lean meat with slow‑digesting carbs and fiber to fuel training in a deficit.

Lean • Complex carbs

Chicken + Rice + Olive Oil

Classic cut: measured carbs with a drizzle of healthy fats to keep meals satisfying.

Balanced

Tofu Veggie Stir‑fry

Plant‑based protein with high‑volume veggies and light sauce for flavor without excess calories.

Plant protein
  • Cutting emphasizes lean proteins and complex carbs like oats, rice, quinoa, potatoes. [8][14]
  • Maintain muscle by keeping protein high during calorie restriction. [8]
